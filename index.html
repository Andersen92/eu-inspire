cooperation

The European Union is one of the key donors and development actors in Kyrgyzstan.

The EU's bilateral, regional and thematic programmes contribute to sustainable and inclusive development of the country on all levels. The programmes support the work of the government, civil society, private sector and other actors, and improve the resilience and prosperity of the Kyrgyz people.
