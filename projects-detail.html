“Promoting Energy and Resource Efficiency in the Tourism Industry of Kyrgyzstan” (PERETO)

Project description

The goal of the project is to promote energy security and sustainable growth by promoting sustainable production and consumption (SCP) and energy and resource efficiency (ERE) practices among small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the tourism sector of Kyrgyzstan. The project is designed for four years from 2020 to 2024, with the funding from the European Union under the SWITCH-Asia Programme. The project is being implemented by the American University of Central Asia (AUCA) in consortium with partner organizations: UNISON Group (Kyrgyzstan), Technopolis Group (Belgium), Collaborating Center on Sustainable Consumption and Production – СSCP (Germany). TARGET GROUPS: HoReCa SMEs and tourism associations, financial institutions, technology suppliers, public institutions, tourists and consumers, educational institutions.

Specific objective

  • raising awareness among consumers and SMEs in the tourism sector about SCP and ERE;
  • promotion of private sector initiatives aimed at developing and achieving the goals of a green economy in the Kyrgyz Republic;
  • development of new green finance products adapted to the needs of SMEs in the tourism sector;
  • promoting national dialogue and policy development on SCP and ERE implementation.

Key findings/progresses

  • PERETO project consultants advised 740 entrepreneurs from the hospitality and tourism sector on sustainable production and consumption, as well as energy and resource efficiency.
  • PERETO launched free energy efficiency consultations in Bishkek for HoReCa and tourism entrepreneurs. The main goal is to help mitigate the negative impact of the energy crisis on the sector caused by the shortage of coal, fuel and lubricants and rolling blackouts in the winter season
  • PERETO, together with the HoReCa Club Association, has developed a “COVID Free Guide” for businesses to help recover from the pandemic
  • The project organized a series of online discussions on the topic “Problems and Prospects for the Implementation of Energy and Resource Efficient Technologies in Tourism” with the participation of government agencies, businessmen, travel associations, suppliers of “green” technologies and the banking sector
  • The project developed and presented the study “Financing sustainable consumption and production, energy and resource efficiency in the tourism industry of the Kyrgyz Republic”.
  • PERETO project partners “Unison Group” achieved amendments to the laws “On Energy Saving” and “On Energy Efficiency of Buildings” of the Kyrgyz Republic, which were approved by the Parliament of the Kyrgyz Republic
  • The project developed a guide for using the Self-Assessment Tool (SAT). This is an online platform with which entrepreneurs can evaluate their business conduct in terms of sustainable (responsible) production and consumption, as well as energy and resource efficiency
  • PERETO launched the PERETO Library on the website, where it collected best practices on saving water, electricity, waste prevention used in the world and KR in the HoReCa and tourism sector
